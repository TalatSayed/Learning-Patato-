FAQs

Everything you need to know.

Do you provide home tuition across Pune?

Yes, we provide home tuition across Pune, Pimpri-Chinchwad and nearby areas.

Are online classes 1-on-1 or group?

Both are available. Most students prefer 1-on-1 for faster results.

What are the tuition fees?

Fees depend on class, board and subject. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How are tutors selected?

Every tutor goes through background verification, academic evaluation, demo class and continuous parent feedback monitoring.

Which boards do you cover?

CBSE, ICSE, SSC, State Board, IB, IGCSE, ISC and NIOS.

Which subjects do you teach?

Maths, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Accountancy, Economics, Coding, French, Computer Science and more.

Can I get a free demo class?

Yes! Book a free demo via the contact form, call or WhatsApp.

Can I change my tutor if needed?

Absolutely. We'll arrange a replacement at no extra cost if needed.

Do you offer exam & Olympiad preparation?

Yes, we offer board, entrance and Olympiad preparation.

Usually within 24–72 hours of confirming your requirements.
